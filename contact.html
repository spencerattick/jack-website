Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What types of projects do you take on?

I work on a variety of IT projects including cloud migrations, infrastructure modernization, security assessments, DevOps implementation, and technology strategy consulting. Projects can range from short-term assessments to long-term engagements.

What is your typical engagement process?

Every engagement starts with a discovery call to understand your needs and challenges. From there, I provide a detailed proposal outlining scope, timeline, and investment. Once approved, we kick off with a thorough discovery phase before moving into implementation.

Do you work with remote clients?

Absolutely! While I'm based in the San Francisco Bay Area, I work with clients across the country and internationally. Modern collaboration tools make remote engagements highly effective.

What are your rates?

Rates vary depending on the scope and complexity of the project. I offer both hourly consulting and project-based pricing. Let's discuss your needs to determine the best arrangement for your situation.
